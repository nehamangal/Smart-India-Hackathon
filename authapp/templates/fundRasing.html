Fund Raising

understanding investors:

• Investors are not in the business of making your business work; they are in the business of entering & exiting the business. o They raise money from LPs (Limited Partner) o When they raise this money, they promise a return to them. o They enter the business with potential growth, make capital gains & exit. o If your company doesn't grow they would urge for a merger, or selling it since they need to get their returns.

What to ask yourself before raising money?

• When is the right time to raise the money?
• What to do with that money?
• You need something to show the investor that their money will grow, do you have that ‘something’?
