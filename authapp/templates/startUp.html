Startup

A start up is finding a way to generate revenue by solving a problem. It is different from conventional businesses in terms of Innovation, Investment, Risk and Creativity. These problems can be solved with the help of ideas which come either from Surrounding or from the issues that you yourself are facing or are watching someone face.

Why to go for start ups?

Start ups or Entrepreneurship in general has its own benefits: • Contribution to the economy • Solving Problem • Interpersonal Skill Development • Experience and Growth • Market and organization knowledge

How to start?

• Idea Validation
• Research
• Prototype
• Market Launch Strategy
• Execution
