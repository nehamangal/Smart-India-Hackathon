Pitch Deck

What is a Pitch Deck?

A pitch deck is a communication tool used to provide audience with a overview of our business. You use your pitch deck during face-to-face or online meetings with potential investors to make them believe that your startup is viable and worth investing.

What includes in a Pitch Deck?

There is no fixed format for a pitch deck but surely there are certain topics which a pitch deck must cover, namely: • Introduction
• Problem Statement
• Solution
• Market size and opportunity
• Product
• Traction
• Competition
• Financials
• Investment ask and fund utilization
• Team
