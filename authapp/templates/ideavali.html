Idea Selection and Validation

There is a funnel that needs to be followed in order to start up. In the funnel, the first step is to select an idea and to get it validated.
Idea selection is the process of shortlisting the believably right idea to work on, which leads to understanding if the idea is actually workable.

Ideas are shortlisted on the basis of:

• Feasibility
• Desirability
• Viability
