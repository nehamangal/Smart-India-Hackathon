REVENUE MODELS

Revenue modeling has a direct impact on the success of a business and its everyday processes. Anyone who has strategic input in a company's operation should understand the revenue model and be able to explain it to their team to guide their efforts. It identifies the potential of different sources of cash flow to grow over time and earn increasing profits for a business. Revenue modeling also helps align marketing efforts with the characteristics and behaviors of certain customer segments.

There are various types of Revenue models like:

• Ad-Based Revenue Model
• Affiliate Revenue Model
• Transactional Revenue Model
• Subscription Revenue Model
• Ad-Based Revenue Model
